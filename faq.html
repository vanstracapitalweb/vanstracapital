Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Vanstra Capital's banking services, account opening, security, and more.

How do I open an account with Vanstra Capital?

Opening an account with Vanstra Capital is simple and takes less than 5 minutes. Click the "Open Account" button, provide your email address, personal information, and upload a valid ID. We'll verify your identity and send you account details within 24 hours.

What documents do I need to open an account?

You'll need a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID) and your Social Security number. For business accounts, additional documentation like business formation documents and tax ID numbers may be required.

Is there a minimum deposit requirement?

Most of our accounts have no minimum deposit requirement to open. However, some specialized accounts like Money Market ($2,500 minimum) and Wealth Management ($100,000 minimum) have specific requirements to access their premium features and rates.

How secure is my money with Vanstra Capital?

Your deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, multi-factor authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring. Our systems are SOC 2 certified and undergo regular security audits by independent firms.

What should I do if I suspect fraud on my account?

Contact us immediately at 1-800-VANSTRA or through your mobile app. Our fraud team is available 24/7. We'll freeze your account if necessary, investigate the suspicious activity, and work with you to secure your account and recover any unauthorized transactions.

What types of accounts does Vanstra Capital offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of accounts including High-Yield Savings (4.85% APY), Premium Checking, Money Market accounts, CDs, business banking solutions, and wealth management services. Each account is designed to maximize your financial potential.

Can I access my accounts on mobile devices?

Yes! Our mobile app is available for iOS and Android devices. You can check balances, transfer funds, deposit checks, pay bills, and access all your account features. The app includes biometric login and real-time notifications for complete security and convenience.

How long do transfers take?

Internal transfers between your Vanstra Capital accounts are instant. ACH transfers to external accounts typically take 1-2 business days. Wire transfers are same-day if initiated before 3 PM ET. We also offer instant transfers to participating banks through our real-time payment network.

Are there limits on transfers?

Daily transfer limits vary by account type and verification status. Basic accounts have a $10,000 daily limit, while verified premium accounts can transfer up to $100,000 per day. Higher limits are available for business and wealth management clients. Wire transfers have separate limits.

What fees does Vanstra Capital charge?

We pride ourselves on transparency and low fees. Most personal accounts have no monthly maintenance fees. Our High-Yield Savings has no minimum balance requirements. ATM fees are reimbursed up to $20/month. Wire transfers and international services have competitive fees clearly displayed before processing.

How is interest calculated and paid?

Interest is calculated daily and compounded monthly for most accounts. Our High-Yield Savings currently offers 4.85% APY, with rates subject to change based on market conditions. Interest is typically credited to your account on the last business day of each month. All rates are clearly displayed in your account dashboard.

Can I have multiple accounts?

Yes! You can open multiple accounts including savings, checking, and investment accounts. Many customers maintain both a High-Yield Savings for emergency funds and a Premium Checking for daily transactions. You can easily transfer funds between your accounts instantly and at no cost.

How do I set up two-factor authentication?

Two-factor authentication can be set up in your account settings under "Security." You can choose SMS verification, authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy), or biometric authentication if your device supports it. We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for maximum account security.

Can't find what you're looking for?

Our customer support team is here to help. Contact us directly for personalized assistance with your specific questions or concerns.
